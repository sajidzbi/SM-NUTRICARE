✨ KEY FEATURES
🔬
USDA Verified Nutrition Data
Fetches authentic data from USDA FoodData Central API — the gold standard in nutrition science worldwide
🇵🇰
Pakistani Food Database
60+ Pakistani foods (Biryani, Nihari, Haleem, Roti, Dal & more) with USDA-verified nutritional values
✏️
Custom Food Entry
Add any food manually. Auto-fills nutrition from USDA API. Edit values freely. Save to "My Foods" for reuse
⭐
My Foods Library
Save custom foods and reuse anytime. Edit or delete them. Original default data never changes
🔄
Reset to Default
Remove all custom entries and restore original database with one tap — confirmation required before reset
📊
Smart Reports & Health Score
Daily health score (0–100), calorie deficit/surplus, weekly chart, BMI, TDEE & personalized tips
🏃
Activity-Based Calorie Burn
TDEE = BMR × Activity multiplier. Shows daily calories burned based on your lifestyle level
📴
Offline-First Design
Full local database always available. Works without internet. Background USDA sync when online
📲
Install as App (PWA)
Install directly from Chrome browser — works like a native app on Android & iOS home screen
🌙
Dark & Light Mode
Toggle between Night (dark) and Day (light) theme. Your preference is saved automatically
🌐
Bilingual (EN / اردو)
Full Urdu & English support. Header and footer always remain in English. Content switches language
💧
Water Intake Tracker
Track daily water intake (up to 8 glasses). Tap each glass to log. Included in health score
📐
BMI Calculator
Auto-calculates BMI from your profile. Visual indicator with Underweight/Normal/Overweight/Obese scale
📡 Data Source: USDA FoodData Central (fdc.nal.usda.gov) — Free US government API, CC0 licensed, science-verified, updated quarterly.
🔢 Calorie Formula: BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor) × Activity Level (1.2–1.9) = TDEE. Accounts for age, gender, height & weight.
